Call for Workshop and Special Session Proposal

The AITM2021 organizing Committee invites proposals for the Workshops and Special Sessions Programs of AITM2021.
These workshops and special sessions will be held both during the main conference.
A Workshop or Special Session will consist of a number of papers in a specific discipline related to the main topics of AITM2021

Submission Guidelines

The organizers of approved workshops are expected to define the workshop's aim, gather and review submissions.
Workshop or special session proposals should be submitted in PDF format.
The format of papers submitted to the workshops and must follow the general template of the conference paper format.

Proposals for Workshops or Special Sessions must include:

1. Title of the event, i.e. the workshop or special session
2. Rationale of behind the event and its objectives
3. Specific topics of interest
4. Contact information (E-mail address and Phone Number) for the event chair(s)
5. Short biography of the event chair(s)

Submission details:

1. Workshop and Special Session proposal deadline
2. Notification of Workshop and Special session acceptance
3. Workshop and Special Session paper submission deadline Same of the main conf
4. Notification of paper acceptance Same of the main conf
5. Camera-ready paper submission deadline Same of the main conf
